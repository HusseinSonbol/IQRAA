Infinite Powers: How Calculus Reveals the Secrets of the Universe

Author: Steven Strogatz

Infinite Powers recounts how calculus tantalized and thrilled its inventors , starting with its first glimmers in ancient Greece and bringing us right up to the discovery of gravitational waves ( a phenomenon predicted by calculus ) . As Strogatz proves , calculus is truly the language of the universe . By unveiling the principles of that language , Infinite Powers makes us marvel at the world anew .
